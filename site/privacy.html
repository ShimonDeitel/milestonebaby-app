Privacy Policy

Milestone

Milestone is an offline-first app. All entries, photos, and notes you create are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or entry content with us or any third party.

The only network connection the app makes is to Apple's App Store servers to process subscription purchases via StoreKit. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction data.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it. There is no account, no login, and no server-side backup.
